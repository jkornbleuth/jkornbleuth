Amatriciana

The First Known Written Recipe of Tomato Sauce for Pasta in 1790

Created in Amatrice, Italy and made famous in Rome

What's in it?

Peeled Tomatoes

Guanciale (Pork Cheek)

Spaghetti or Bucatini

Pecorino Romano

Salt & Pepper

I discovered Amatriciana while studying in Rome last year and the flavor was amazing. It was my first time trying pork cheek and it goes extremely well with the tomato sauce and pasta.
